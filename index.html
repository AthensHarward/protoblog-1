The Muse and the Machine

Welcome to a space where antiquity and modernity converge. Dive into discussions where we explore the impacts of technology through the lens of timeless wisdom.

As we are standing at the threshold of a new world, it is important that we make time to self-reflect before we take that finalizing step through the doorway. Even if we are successful at speculating about what lies ahead of us, we need to make sure we understand its true significance and what it means to our humanity. If we fail to acknowledge where we came from and what our true nature and values are, then we risk losing the beauty of our heritage. It is true that there is immense pain and suffering in our past. There is also altruism, sacrifice, authenticity, friendship, and the ever-deepening pursuit of finding meaning. Just as humanity is capable of intense evil, it is just as capable of good. And I may argue that evil is a direction with a built-in ending and results only in destruction, whereas good is something that grows and has no upper limits.

For the first time in human history, we are approaching an opportunity where the best parts of us can truly take hold and lift us out of the world of deceit and oppression. The world of abundance and enlightenment that will become available to each one of us will give us unprecedented power to root out the hidden atrocities and the overt violence that has been inflicted on the masses for millennia.

The purpose of this blog is to explore timeless wisdom and hopefully, get that much closer to the true essence of us. If we pursue who we truly are, I foresee the discovery of the good within us. It is paramount that we take this good and use it as the guiding principles as we step through this final door. It is possibly the only thing in our possession that will get us through this global change. Perhaps it is the only real possession any of us has ever really needed and more than any time in history, we need it now. Good takes effort. Good takes diligence. Good takes a sincere and passionate drive, much like a sapling reaching for the light in a dark forest.
